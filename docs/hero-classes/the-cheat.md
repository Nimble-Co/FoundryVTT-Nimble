# The Cheat

**Key Stats:** DEX, INT | **Hit Die:** 1d6 | **Starting HP:** 10
**Saves:** DEX+, WIL– | **Armor:** Leather Armor | **Weapons:** DEX Weapons
**Starting Gear:** 2 Daggers, Sling, Cheap Hides, Chalk

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Sneak Attack — (1/turn) When you crit, deal +1d6 damage.
- [ ] Vicious Opportunist — (1/turn) When you hit a Distracted target with a melee attack, change the Primary Die roll to whatever you like (max = crit).

### Level 2
- [ ] Cheat — (1/round) Move or Hide for free; (1/day) change any skill check to 10+INT; Initiative minimum 10; advantage on games/wagers.

### Level 3
- [ ] Thieves' Cant — Learn the secret language of rogues and scoundrels.

### Level 5
- [ ] Twist the Blade — Action: Change one Sneak Attack die to whatever you like.
- [ ] Quick Read — (1/encounter) Advantage on Assess check; (1/day) advantage on Examination check.

### Level 6
- [ ] THAT's Not What Happened! — (1/Safe Rest) Action: After a Distracted enemy attacks you, change their Primary Die roll to whatever you like (minimum = miss).

### Level 13
- [ ] Twist the Blade (2) — (1/turn) You can Twist the Blade for free.

### Level 20
- [ ] Supreme Execution — +1 to any 2 stats. When attacking with a blade, don't require targets to be Distracted to trigger Vicious Opportunist.

---

## Choice Abilities — Underhanded Abilities

- [ ] "Creative" Accounting — Steal up to INT actions from your next turn (gain now, subtract later). Cannot use 2 turns in a row.
- [ ] Exploit Weakness — Action: Contested INT check vs. enemy; on win, use Vicious Opportunist against them even if not Distracted (1 min or until retargeted).
- [ ] Feinting Attack — If you miss for the 2nd time in a single round, change the primary die roll to any result.
- [ ] How'd YOU get here?! — 2 actions: "Teleport" up to 4 spaces adjacent to a Distracted target and attack. If you crit, teleport again.
- [ ] I'm Outta Here! — When an ally within 4 spaces is crit, turn Invisible until end of next turn, then move half speed for free.
- [ ] Misdirection — Gain INT armor. Whenever you Defend, halve the damage instead.
- [ ] Steal Tempo — When you land a critical hit for the 2nd time on a turn, target loses 1 action and you gain 1 action.
- [ ] Sunder Armor (Medium) — Action: When you crit an enemy with medium armor, sunder it; until start of next turn, ALL melee attacks against that target ignore armor.
- [ ] Sunder Armor (Heavy) — Req. Sunder Armor (Medium). Extends to heavy armor enemies.
- [ ] Trickshot — When you throw a dagger, it returns at end of turn. On hit, ricochets to another creature within 2 spaces for half damage.

---

## Subclass: Silent Blade

### Level 3
- [ ] Amidst All This Commotion… — If a creature dies while you Sneak Attack them, turn Invisible until you attack again or until start of next turn.
- [ ] Leave No Trace — Advantage on Stealth checks at full health.

### Level 7
- [ ] Cunning Strike — (2/encounter) When you land a Sneak Attack, force STR save (DC 10+INT); on failure, deal max Sneak Attack damage instead of rolling.

### Level 11
- [ ] Professional Skulker — Gain climbing speed and advantage on Stealth checks (replaces Leave No Trace).

### Level 15
- [ ] KILL — When you crit an enemy with fewer max HP than you, it dies.

---

## Subclass: Scoundrel

### Level 3
- [ ] Low Blow — When you Sneak Attack, spend 2 extra actions to Incapacitate target for their next turn on failed STR save (DC 10+INT). Regardless of save, they are Taunted by you until you drop to 0 HP.
- [ ] Sweet Talk — Advantage on Influence checks with NPCs you've just met for the first time (until you fail or meet a 2nd time).

### Level 7
- [ ] Pocket Sand — (2/encounter) When you Defend against a melee attack, Blind the attacker until start of their next turn and force them to reroll.

### Level 11
- [ ] Escape Plan — (1/Safe Rest) When you would drop to 0 HP or gain a Wound, you don't; turn Invisible for 1 minute or until you attack.

### Level 15
- [ ] Heads I Win, Tails You Lose — (1/encounter) Attacks this round don't miss; crit on 1 less; gain LVL temp HP.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Incapacitated — Low Blow (Scoundrel L3)
- Taunted — Low Blow (Scoundrel L3)
- Blinded — Pocket Sand (Scoundrel L7)
- Invisible — I'm Outta Here!, Amidst All This Commotion… (Silent Blade L3), Escape Plan (Scoundrel L11)
