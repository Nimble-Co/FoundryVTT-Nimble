# Shadowmancer

**Key Stats:** INT, DEX | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13
**Saves:** INT+, WIL– | **Armor:** Cloth Armor | **Weapons:** Blades, Wands
**Starting Gear:** Adventurer's Garb, Sickle, Shovel

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Shadow Blast — (Necrotic cantrip, 1/turn) Action: Range 8, Damage 1d12+KEY. High Levels: +1d12 every 5 levels.
- [ ] Summon Shadows — (Necrotic cantrip) Action: Summon a shadow minion within Reach 1 (max INT or LVL, whichever is lower). Action: (1/turn) Command ALL minions to move 6 then attack (Reach 1, 1d12 each). High Levels: +1 Reach every 5 levels.

### Level 2
- [ ] Master of Darkness — Know Necrotic cantrips and tier 1 spells.
- [ ] Pilfered Power — Steal power from patron to cast tiered spells (always at highest tier unlocked). DEX times before patron takes notice; each excess: patron deals half max HP. Resets on Safe Rest communion.

### Level 3
- [ ] Lesser Shadow Invocation — Choose 1 Lesser Shadow Invocation.

### Level 4
- [ ] Greater Shadow Invocation — Choose 1 Greater Shadow Invocation.

### Level 5
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 6
- [ ] Shadowmastery — Choose 1 Necrotic Utility Spell.

### Level 12
- [ ] Greedy Pact — When Pilfered Power would damage you, make a STR save: 1–9 = full damage; 10–19 = 10 HP damage; 20+ = no damage and cast as if 1 tier higher.

### Level 17
- [ ] Dire Shadows — Attacks against shadow minions made with disadvantage. They take no damage from successful saves.

### Level 20
- [ ] Eldritch Usurper — +1 to any 2 stats. Whenever you summon a single shadow minion, summon 2 instead. They die only when receiving 12+ damage at one time.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

---

## Choice Abilities — Lesser Invocations

- [ ] Abhorrent Speech — Communicate with horrible creatures (aberrations, undead, etc.).
- [ ] Beguiling Influence — (1/day) Reroll an Influence check.
- [ ] Blood Sight — (1/day) Reroll an Examination check. Detect traces of blood on a surface even after cleaned.
- [ ] Devoted Acolyte — Learn 2 languages (Celestial, Draconic, Deep Speak, Infernal, or Primordial). Advantage on related Lore checks.
- [ ] Eldritch Sense — Sense the presence of any shapechanger or magically concealed creature within 6 spaces.
- [ ] Gaze of Two Minds — Touch a willing creature and perceive through its senses for as long as you hold concentration.
- [ ] Knowledge from Beyond — Whenever you fail an Insight or Arcana check, suffer 1 Wound to succeed instead.
- [ ] My Favored Pet — One shadow minion tolerates you outside combat; can do menial tasks.
- [ ] Voice of the Dark — Communicate telepathically with a humanoid within 6 spaces.
- [ ] Whispers of the Grave — (1/day) Ask a dead creature 3 yes/no questions. Cannot be questioned again this way.

---

## Choice Abilities — Greater Invocations

- [ ] Armor of Shadows — Reduce all damage received by the number of minions you have.
- [ ] Fiendish Boon — Increase DEX or INT by 1. You have 1 fewer maximum Hit Dice.
- [ ] Hungering Shadows — Whenever one of your shadows would crit, the next tiered spell cast this encounter doesn't cost a Pilfered Power use.
- [ ] One with Shadows — Action: When in dim light or darkness, become Invisible until you move or attack.
- [ ] Repelling Blast — When you hit a Medium or smaller creature with Shadow Blast, push it up to 2 spaces away.
- [ ] Shadow Magus — Your minions gain +4 Reach and deal d10 damage instead.
- [ ] Shadow Rush — When shadow minions attack, instead of rolling damage, have any of them deal max amount then die.
- [ ] Shadow Spear — Shadow Blast targets creatures twice as far away, ignores cover, and has advantage against Prone targets.
- [ ] Shadow Warp — Action: Switch places with a creature within 12 spaces dealt necrotic damage this turn.
- [ ] Swarming Shadows — Whenever one of your shadows would crit, summon another shadow minion adjacent to the target.
- [ ] Vengeful Blast — Whenever a minion dies, cast Shadow Blast as a reaction (even if already cast this turn).

---

## Subclass: Pact of the Red Dragon

### Level 3
- [ ] Draconic Crimson Rite — Know Fire spells. Shadow minions become flaming dragon wyrmling shadows. Shadow Blast and minions can deal fire or necrotic damage, inflicting Smoldering whenever they would crit.

### Level 7
- [ ] We'll ALL Burn! — Cast Pyroclasm without Pilfering Power by including yourself in the damage. Advantage on the save. Choose 1 Fire Utility Spell.

### Level 11
- [ ] Heart of Burning Fire — Regain 1 use of Pilfered Power each time you roll Initiative (expires at end of combat).

### Level 15
- [ ] Enveloped by the Master — Gain 1d4 Wounds to cast Dragonform.

---

## Subclass: Pact of the Abyssal Depths

### Level 3
- [ ] Master of Nightfrost — Know Ice spells. Gain the ability to breathe underwater. Shadow minions become beings of nightfrost. Shadow Blast and minions can deal cold or necrotic damage; whenever they would crit, gain INT+LVL temp HP.

### Level 7
- [ ] Shadowfrost — Shadow Blast also Slows. Cast Cryosleep or Rimeblades without Pilfering Power by expending 10 temp HP. Choose 1 Ice Utility Spell.

### Level 11
- [ ] Glacial Resilience — (1/Safe Rest) Reaction (whenever attacked or would gain a condition): Gain 10×LVL temp HP and end ALL negative conditions. At end of next turn, remaining temp HP are lost.

### Level 15
- [ ] Cryomancer's Reprisal — Pay half max HP to cast ANY Ice spell. After casting this way, gain an invisible aura: next creature to hit you in melee this encounter takes cold damage equal to half the HP spent.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Smoldering — Draconic Crimson Rite crits (Red Dragon L3)
- Slowed — Shadowfrost (Abyssal Depths L7)
- Invisible — One with Shadows (Greater Invocation)
