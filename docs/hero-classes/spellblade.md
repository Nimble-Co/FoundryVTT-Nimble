# Spellblade

**Base Class:** Commander (Story Subclass — replaces existing subclass)
**Story Trigger:** Steel meets spell — arcane Commander

---

## Base Class Changes (from Commander)

- **Arcane Command** — Lose access to Weapon Mastery and Combat Tactics. Gain INT mana when you roll Initiative (lost if unspent when combat ends). Whenever you could choose a Combat Tactic or Weapon Mastery, instead choose another Commander's Order or a tier 1 (or lower) spell from any spell school.

Commander's Orders are **empowered with magical power**:

- [ ] Face Me! → **Glimmering Decree** — Reaction (after an ally within 12 spaces is crit): That enemy takes STR d8 radiant damage (ignoring armor), is pulled up to 4 spaces toward you, and is Taunted by you until you drop to 0 HP.
- [ ] Move it! Move it! → **Borne upon the Wind** — When you roll Initiative, give yourself and an ally advantage on the roll, +3 speed, and the ability to fly for 1 round. Both also move for free.
- [ ] Hold the Line! → **Crystalline Armor** — (1/encounter) Reaction (when an ally drops to 0 HP): Set their HP to 3×LVL and grant that many temp HP. Enemies who reduce this temp HP in melee have their speed halved until end of their next turn.
- [ ] Reposition! → **Flashstep** — Action/Reaction (on an ally's turn): Command 1 ally to move up to their speed (or 2 allies half speed) for free. You may exchange places with one of them.
- [ ] I Can Do This ALL DAY! → **Rising Phoenix** — (1/encounter) Reaction (when you would drop to 0 HP): Expend Hit Dice, set HP to sum rolled, and deal that much fire damage to each enemy within 2 spaces. They gain the Smoldering condition.
- [ ] Coordinated Strike! → **Withering Strike** — Any attacks made this way deal additional Necrotic damage equal to the max value of your Combat Die. An enemy damaged this way is considered undead for 1 round.

## Subclass Abilities (by level)

### Level 3
- [ ] Firebrand — When you roll Initiative, cast Enchant Weapon for free (can be upcast by spending additional mana).
- [ ] Deep Knowledge (1) — Choose any tier 1 (or lower) spell and any Utility Spell.

### Level 7
- [ ] Deep Knowledge (2) — Choose any tier 2 (or lower) spell and any Utility Spell.

### Level 11
- [ ] Deep Knowledge (3) — Choose any tier 3 (or lower) spell and any Utility Spell.

### Level 15
- [ ] Deep Knowledge (4) — Choose any tier 4 (or lower) spell and any Utility Spell.

## Conditions Applied

- Taunted — Glimmering Decree (empowered Face Me!)
- Smoldering — Rising Phoenix (empowered I Can Do This ALL DAY!)
- Speed halved — Crystalline Armor (empowered Hold the Line!)
