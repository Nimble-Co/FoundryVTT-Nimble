# Zephyr

**Key Stats:** DEX, STR | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13
**Saves:** DEX+, INT– | **Armor:** None | **Weapons:** Melee
**Starting Gear:** Staff, Traveling Robes & Sandals

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Iron Defense — Armor equals DEX+STR as long as you are unarmored.
- [ ] Swift Fists — Unarmed strikes not subject to Rushed Attack disadvantage. Damage: 1d4+STR.

### Level 2
- [ ] Swift Feet — While unarmored, gain +2 speed and +LVL Initiative.
- [ ] Burst of Speed — When you roll Initiative, gain DEX Bursts of Speed for that encounter. (1/turn) Spend 1 Burst of Speed to use one of: **Slipstream** (Defend; attack misses), **Whirling Defense** (Defend and apply armor to every attack this round), **Swiftstrike** (attack on your turn, ignoring Rushed Attack disadvantage), or **Windstep** (move on your turn, ignoring difficult terrain).

### Level 3
- [ ] Kinetic Momentum — Whenever you gain a Wound, gain a Burst of Speed.
- [ ] Ethereal Projection — (1/day) Meditate 10+ min to project an ethereal version of yourself up to 30 ft. away, passing through solid objects. See through its eyes. Lasts up to 10 minutes.

### Level 4
- [ ] Unyielding Resolve — Ignore the first Wound you would suffer each encounter (when-Wounded abilities still trigger).
- [ ] Martial Master — Choose a Martial Arts ability.

### Level 5
- [ ] Reverberating Strikes — Add LVL bludgeoning damage to all melee attacks.

### Level 6
- [ ] Infuse Strength — Action: Make an unarmed strike against an ally and infuse them with strength instead of harming them. Expend Hit Dice and heal them as you would during a Field Rest.

### Level 9
- [ ] Swift Feet (2) — Gain an additional +2 speed while unarmored.

### Level 10
- [ ] Unyielding Resolve (2) — Ignore the first 2 Wounds you would suffer each encounter.

### Level 13
- [ ] Iron Defense (2) — Your armor is doubled while unarmored.

### Level 17
- [ ] Unyielding Resolve (3) — Ignore the first 3 Wounds each encounter. Advantage on STR saves while Dying.

### Level 20
- [ ] Windborne — +1 to any 2 stats. +1 additional Burst of Speed when you roll Initiative. Permanently gain 1 action (while Dying, max 2 actions).

---

## Choice Abilities — Martial Arts

- [ ] Airshift — Cannot be Grappled while conscious. While moving, travel across all terrain as normal ground, ignoring all ill effects (walls, ceilings, water, lava, etc.).
- [ ] Blur — (1/encounter) When you Defend, move up to half your speed away first; take no damage if now out of range or at Full Cover.
- [ ] Bodily Discipline — Spend 1 action to end any non-Wound condition on yourself.
- [ ] Enduring Soul — Each time you roll Initiative, gain Hit Dice equal to the actions you get on your first turn. Expire at end of combat if unused.
- [ ] I Jump On His Back! — While moving with Windstep, if you move into the space of a creature your size or larger, jump onto its back. Gain advantage on melee attacks against it; any damage you avoid is dealt to it instead.
- [ ] Kinetic Barrage — Whenever you miss an attack, gain a cumulative +STR bonus to all damage for the rest of this encounter.
- [ ] Mighty Soul — Cannot be moved against your will. Whenever you would fail a saving throw, gain a Wound to add your STR to the result (repeat any number of times).
- [ ] Quickstrike — When you Interpose, make a free unarmed strike against the enemy first.
- [ ] Use Momentum — Whenever you avoid all damage from a melee attack (miss or Defend), swap places with the attacker and choose another target now within the attack's reach; they are hit instead.
- [ ] Vital Rejuvenation — When you receive healing for the first time on a turn, heal another target within 6 spaces HP equal to your STR.
- [ ] Windstrider — If you move through the space of a willing creature while using Windstep, they can move with you and end in any space adjacent to your path.

---

## Subclass: Way of Pain

### Level 3
- [ ] Bring the Pain — (1/round) Turn any melee attack against you into a crit. Whenever you are crit, reduce damage by half; the attacker takes the same amount (ignoring armor). May suffer 1 Wound to double the damage the enemy takes.

### Level 7
- [ ] Share My Pain — Swiftstrike can also target a 2nd creature within Reach 2.

### Level 11
- [ ] Pain Sharpens the Mind — While Bloodied, advantage on first attack each turn and on all saves.

### Level 15
- [ ] Echoed Agony — Swiftstrike can also target a 3rd creature within Reach 4.

---

## Subclass: Way of Flame

### Level 3
- [ ] Exploding Soul — (1/round) On your turn, suffer a Wound. Whenever you gain a Wound, deal STR+Wounds damage to chosen creatures within 2 spaces (ignoring armor) and give them the Smoldering condition.

### Level 7
- [ ] Blazing Speed — +2 speed while using Windstep. After you cease movement with Windstep, enemies you passed through take STR+DEX fire damage. Smoldering enemies take double, ending the condition.

### Level 11
- [ ] Chain Reaction — (1/turn) When you crit, deal fire damage equal to STR+Wounds to creatures of your choice within 2 spaces of your target. Repeat targeting creatures within 2 spaces of any already damaged.

### Level 15
- [ ] Burning Soul — Double any fire damage you deal.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Grappled immunity — Airshift (Martial Arts)
- Smoldering — Exploding Soul (Way of Flame L3), Blazing Speed targets (Way of Flame L7)
