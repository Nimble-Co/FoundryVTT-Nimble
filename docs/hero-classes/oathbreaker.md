# Oathbreaker

**Base Class:** Oathsworn (Story Subclass — replaces existing subclass)
**Story Trigger:** Fallen from the light, seeking redemption

---

## Base Class Features (retained from Oathsworn)

See [`oathsworn.md`](oathsworn.md) for full core ability list. The following changes apply:

- **Dark Benediction** — Lose access to: True Strike, Heal, Warding Bond. Gain access to: Entice, Shadowtrap, Dread Visage. May choose Radiant or Necrotic Utility Spells.
- **Paragon of Power** (replaces Paragon of Virtue) — Advantage on Might checks when attempting to intimidate others.
- **Aura of Suffering** — Gain an aura with Reach 4 and can Interpose for an ally anywhere within your aura. Radiant Judgment no longer triggers when attacked — instead triggers whenever you could Interpose but don't.

---

## Subclass Abilities (by level)

### Level 3
- [ ] We All Suffer — Gain +2 max Wounds. When an ally within your aura would gain any Wounds or fail a save, you may suffer the effect instead and trigger Radiant Judgment.
- [ ] Bring Me Your Pain — Reaction (when a willing ally within your aura would drop to 0 HP): Switch HP with them (if your current HP exceeds their max HP, they gain temp HP equal to the difference), dropping to 0 HP and gaining the Wound instead.

### Level 7
- [ ] Torment — Lay on Hands heals you for twice as much and others for half as much. When you deal damage, expend healing power from Lay on Hands pool to increase damage by the amount spent (ignoring armor).

### Level 11
- [ ] Exploit — Reaction (whenever an ally within your aura Defends): Expend Judgment Dice to force an enemy within your Aura to Interpose (a creature cannot Interpose against its own attack).

### Level 15
- [ ] Bloody Terror — Attacks against you gain 1 instance of disadvantage for each Wound you have (max 3).
