# Shepherd

**Key Stats:** WIL, STR | **Hit Die:** 1d10 | **Starting HP:** 17
**Saves:** WIL+, DEX– | **Armor:** Mail Armor, Shields | **Weapons:** STR Weapons, Wands
**Starting Gear:** Rusty Mail, Mace, Wooden Buckler, Bell

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Keeper of Life & Death — You know Radiant and Necrotic cantrips.
- [ ] Searing Light — (WIL times/Safe Rest) Action: Heal WIL d8 HP to a Dying creature within Reach 6, OR inflict WIL d8 radiant damage to an undead or Bloodied enemy within Reach 6.

### Level 2
- [ ] Mana — Unlock tier 1 Radiant and Necrotic spells. Mana pool = (WIL×3)+LVL; recharges on Safe Rest.
- [ ] Lifebinding Spirit — (Radiant Spell, Tier 1) Action: Summon a spirit companion immune to harm (lasts until recast, Safe Rest, or heals a number of times equal to mana spent). Action: It attacks or heals a creature within Reach 4 for 1d6+WIL radiant damage (ignoring armor) or healing. Upcasting: increment die size by 1 (max d12), +1 healing use.

### Level 3
- [ ] Master of Twilight — Choose 1 Necrotic and 1 Radiant Utility Spell.

### Level 5
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.
- [ ] Sacred Grace — Choose 2 Sacred Graces.

### Level 6
- [ ] Master of Twilight (2) — Choose a 2nd Necrotic and Radiant Utility Spell.

### Level 10
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 11
- [ ] Master of Twilight (3) — You know all Necrotic and Radiant Utility Spells.

### Level 17
- [ ] Revitalizing Blessing — (1/round) Whenever you roll a 6 or higher on one or more healing die, the target may recover one Wound.

### Level 20
- [ ] Twilight Sage — +1 to any 2 stats. Your Lifebinding Spirit rolls twice as many dice.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

## Choice Abilities — Sacred Graces

- [ ] Assist Me, My Friend! — Whenever you make your first melee attack each round, add your Lifebinding Spirit's damage to the attack.
- [ ] Empowered Companion — Whenever you spend mana to call forth your Lifebinding Spirit, cast it as if you spent 1 additional mana (ignoring tier restrictions). Max die size is now d20.
- [ ] Guiding Spirit — When your Lifebinding Spirit rolls a 6+ on its damage die, the target begins to glow with radiant light. The next attack against that target has advantage.
- [ ] Hasty Companion — +4 Reach for your Lifebinding Spirit. It can also act for free when summoned.
- [ ] Illuminate Soul — Action: A creature within 6 spaces begins to glow with radiant light. For 1 Round, attacks against them are made with advantage or disadvantage (your choice). WIL times/Safe Rest.
- [ ] Light Bearer — Regain 1 use of Searing Light when you roll Initiative (expires if unspent at end of combat).
- [ ] Not Beyond MY Reach — May target creatures dead less than 1 round for healing. For every 10 HP healed this way, recover 1 Wound instead (must heal at least 1 Wound to revive).
- [ ] Vengeful Spirit — Action: Your Lifebinding Spirit transforms into a swirling vortex of radiant light. At end of your turn, damages all enemies within 3 spaces of you, ignoring armor and cover. Lasts rounds equal to healing charges left. Ends early if you summon your spirit again.

## Subclass: Luminary of Mercy

### Level 3
- [ ] Merciful Healing — When an effect caused by you heals a Dying creature, heal twice as much. (1/round) Lifebinding Spirit can act for free while you are Dying.
- [ ] Life is Beautiful — Harmless creatures (butterflies, hummingbirds) are attracted to your presence; flowers bloom more vibrantly nearby.

### Level 7
- [ ] Conduit of Light — When an effect caused by you would heal HP, expend 1 use of Searing Light to also heal (or damage, ignoring armor) another target within 6 spaces for the same amount.

### Level 11
- [ ] Powerful Healer — (WIL times/Safe Rest) Whenever you would roll dice to heal damage, instead heal the max amount or give that many temp HP.

### Level 15
- [ ] Empowered Conduit — Conduit of Light may target 1 additional creature. Regain 1 charge of Searing Light when you roll Initiative (expires if unspent at end of combat).

---

## Subclass: Luminary of Malice

### Level 3
- [ ] Soul Reaper — When you use Searing Light to harm an enemy, make a 2nd enemy within range take the same amount of damage (ignoring armor).
- [ ] Harbinger of Decay — Vibrant colors and smells suppressed near you. Foods spoil faster. You may have your Lifebinding Spirit shift into a deathly version (zombie dog, imp, etc.) with necrotic damage type.

### Level 7
- [ ] Veilwalker's Blessing — (1/Safe Rest) Reaction (when you would drop to 0 HP): Drop to 1 HP instead and force an enemy within 6 spaces to make a STR save. Failure: they become Bloodied, or if already Bloodied, drop to 0 HP.

### Level 11
- [ ] Deathbringer's Touch — Your first melee attack each round against a Bloodied creature is an automatic critical hit. Lifebinding Spirit deals additional damage equal to your STR.

### Level 15
- [ ] Conduit of Death — Veilwalker's Blessing recharges when you roll Initiative. Charge is lost if unspent at end of combat.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Radiant glow / advantage — Guiding Spirit (Sacred Grace), Illuminate Soul (Sacred Grace)
