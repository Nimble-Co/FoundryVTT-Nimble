# Berserker

**Key Stats:** STR, DEX | **Hit Die:** 1d12 | **Starting HP:** 20
**Saves:** STR+, INT– | **Armor:** None | **Weapons:** All STR weapons
**Starting Gear:** Battleaxe, Rations (meat), Rope (50 ft.)

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Rage — (1/turn) Action: Roll a Fury Die (1d4) and set it aside; add to every STR attack. Max KEY Fury Dice; lost when Rage ends.
- [ ] That All You Got?! — When attacked, expend 1+ Fury Dice to reduce damage taken by STR+DEX per die spent.

### Level 2
- [ ] Intensifying Fury — If Raging at the beginning of your turn, roll 1 Fury Die for free.
- [ ] One with the Ancients — (1/Safe Rest) When faced with a directional decision, call upon ancestors to guide you toward the most dangerous path.

### Level 3
- [ ] Bloodlust — Expend 1+ Fury Dice on your turn; move DEX spaces per die spent for free.

### Level 4
- [ ] Enduring Rage — While Dying, Rage automatically for free at the start of your turn; max 2 actions; ignore STR saves to attack.
- [ ] Savage Arsenal — Choose 1 Savage Arsenal ability (see below).

### Level 5
- [ ] Rage (2) — Whenever you Rage, gain 2 Fury Dice instead.

### Level 6
- [ ] Intensifying Fury (2) — Fury Dice are now d6s.

### Level 8
- [ ] Savage Arsenal (3) — Choose a 3rd Savage Arsenal ability.

### Level 9
- [ ] Intensifying Fury (3) — Fury Dice are now d8s.

### Level 13
- [ ] Intensifying Fury (4) — Fury Dice are now d10s.

### Level 17
- [ ] Intensifying Fury (5) — Fury Dice are now d12s.

### Level 18
- [ ] DEEP RAGE — Dropping to 0 HP does not end your Rage.

### Level 20
- [ ] BOUNDLESS RAGE — +1 to any 2 stats. Anytime you roll less than 6 on a Fury Die, change it to 6 instead.

---

## Choice Abilities — Savage Arsenal

- [ ] Death Blow — After dealing damage from a crit, expend any number of Fury Dice; sum and deal double that damage.
- [ ] Deathless Rage — (1/turn) While Dying, suffer 1 Wound to gain 1 action.
- [ ] Eager for Battle — Advantage on Initiative. Move 2×DEX spaces for free on first turn each encounter.
- [ ] Into the Fray — Action: Leap up to 2×DEX spaces toward an enemy; if adjacent to 2+ enemies, make a free attack against 1.
- [ ] Mighty Endurance — Survive 4 additional Wounds before death.
- [ ] MORE BLOOD! — Whenever an enemy crits you, gain 1 Fury Die.
- [ ] Rampage — (1/turn) After landing a hit, treat next attack this turn as if you rolled the same amount.
- [ ] Swift Fury — Whenever you gain 1+ Fury Dice, move up to DEX spaces for free, ignoring difficult terrain.
- [ ] Thunderous Steps — After moving 4+ spaces while Raging, deal STR Bludgeoning damage to all adjacent creatures where you stop.
- [ ] Unstoppable Force — While Dying and Raging, damage causes 1 Wound (instead of 2) and crits inflict 2 Wounds (instead of 3).
- [ ] Whirlwind — 2 actions: Attack ALL targets within your melee weapon's reach.
- [ ] You're Next! — Action: While Raging, make a Might check to demoralize an enemy within Reach 12 (DC: their current HP); on success, they flee.

---

## Subclass: Path of the Mountainheart

### Level 3
- [ ] Stone's Resilience — Whenever you expend Fury Dice to reduce incoming damage, add the die value to the amount reduced.
- [ ] Mountainous Tenacity — For every 10 HP recovered by Hit Dice, may heal 1 Wound instead.

### Level 7
- [ ] Unbreakable — (1/encounter) While Raging, if you would suffer your last Wound or a chosen negative condition, you don't.

### Level 11
- [ ] Titan's Fury — After you miss an attack or are crit by an enemy, Rage for free.

### Level 15
- [ ] Mountain's Endurance — While Dying, if an attack would crit you, the attack is rerolled instead (when-crit abilities still trigger).

---

## Subclass: Path of the Red Mist

### Level 3
- [ ] Blood Frenzy — (1/turn) While Raging, whenever you crit or kill an enemy, change 1 Fury Die to the maximum.
- [ ] Savage Awareness — Advantage on Perception checks to notice/track blood. Blindsight 2 while Raging: ignore Blinded and see through darkness and Invisibility within range.

### Level 7
- [ ] Unstoppable Brutality — While Raging, gain 1 Wound to reroll any attack or save.

### Level 11
- [ ] Opportunistic Frenzy — While Raging, opportunity attacks without disadvantage; trigger whenever an enemy enters melee reach.

### Level 15
- [ ] Onslaught — While Raging, gain +2 speed. (1/round) Move for free.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Blinded — countered by Savage Awareness (Red Mist L3, Blindsight ignores it)
