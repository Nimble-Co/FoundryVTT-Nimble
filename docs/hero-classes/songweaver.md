# Songweaver

**Key Stats:** WIL, INT | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13
**Saves:** WIL+, STR– | **Armor:** Cloth, Leather | **Weapons:** DEX, Wands
**Starting Gear:** Adventurer's Garb, Instrument, Dagger, Mirror

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Wind Spellcasting — Know cantrips from the Wind school and 1 other school of your choice.
- [ ] Vicious Mockery — (Wind cantrip) Action: Range 12, Damage 1d4+INT psychic (ignoring armor). On hit: target is Taunted during their next turn. High Levels: +2 damage every 5 levels.
- [ ] Songweaver's Inspiration — (2×WIL times/Safe Rest) Free Reaction: Allow an ally to reroll a single die for an attack or save (must keep either result).

### Level 2
- [ ] Mana — Unlock tier 1 spells in known schools. Mana pool = (INT×3)+LVL; recharges on Safe Rest.
- [ ] Jack of All Trades — When you Safe Rest, move a skill point as if you just leveled up.
- [ ] Song of Rest — (1/day) Whenever you Field Rest, play a song to allow anyone spending Hit Dice to heal additional HP equal to your WIL.

### Level 3
- [ ] Quick Wit — When you roll Initiative, regain 2 spent uses of Songweaver's Inspiration (expire at end of combat if unused).
- [ ] Windbag — Choose 1 Utility Spell from each spell school you know.

### Level 4
- [ ] Lyrical Weaponry — Choose 1 Lyrical Weaponry ability.

### Level 5
- [ ] A "People" Person — Choose 2 friends you know; summon them temporarily via song (1/Safe Rest each).
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 20
- [ ] I'm So Famous! — +1 to any 2 stats. Songweaver's Inspiration cannot fail (your target succeeds).
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

---

## Choice Abilities — Lyrical Weaponry

- [ ] Heroic Ballad — +2 max Songweaver's Inspiration charges. When used to reroll an ally's attack, also grant +WIL damage on that attack.
- [ ] Inspiring Anthem — (1/encounter) Action: Grant all friendly Dying creatures who can hear you 1 HP and 1 action.
- [ ] Not My Beautiful Faaace! — (1/encounter) When you Defend, force attacker to choose another target on failed WIL save (attack fails if none). If they fail by 5+, they attack themselves. On save, they attack you with disadvantage.
- [ ] Rhapsody of the Normal — When you roll 4+ on Vicious Mockery, spend a Songweaver's Inspiration charge to suppress all special abilities of the target until end of their next turn.
- [ ] Song of Domination — (1/encounter) 2 actions: All enemies within 6 spaces who hear it make a WIL save. Failure: move them up to 6 spaces in any direction and they cannot move on their next turn.

---

## Choice Abilities — A "People" Person

- [ ] Stompy — 3 actions: Summon a huge hill giant for 1 round. Make a DC 10 Influence check: success = moves 6 spaces in your chosen direction; failure = moves toward you. Deals LVL+Influence check damage to everything in path.
- [ ] Mal, the Malevolent Imp — Summon a tiny fiend for 1 night to find dangerous information or "take care" of a problem. Make an Influence check (advantage for mischievous/fun tasks; disadvantage for good/menial tasks).
- [ ] Gran Gran (NOT a hag) — When resting, summon her for 1 hour. She bakes WIL+INT pastries; eating one recovers 1 mana, Hit Die, or Wound. Expire in 10 minutes.
- [ ] Linos, the Everfriendly — Summon a legendary flying (but friendly) creature to transport you and your party anywhere. May request a very large amount of food.

---

## Subclass: Herald of Snark

### Level 3
- [ ] Opportunistic Snark — Reaction (when an enemy within Range 12 misses an attack): Cast Vicious Mockery at them; it deals double damage.

### Level 7
- [ ] Fight Picker — (1/turn) When an enemy is damaged by your Vicious Mockery, have one ally Taunt them until end of that enemy's turn.

### Level 11
- [ ] Chord of Chaos — (1/encounter) Action: Move ALL creatures within hearing of your song up to 3 spaces (cannot move into obviously dangerous places).

### Level 15
- [ ] Words Like Swords — Vicious Mockery damage becomes 1d6+INT+WIL.

---

## Subclass: Herald of Courage

### Level 3
- [ ] Inspiring Presence — Whenever you use Songweaver's Inspiration, allies within 12 spaces who can hear you gain WIL temp HP.

### Level 7
- [ ] Unfailing Courage — Songweaver's Inspiration allows your target to roll with advantage.

### Level 11
- [ ] Fire in my Bones — Songweaver's Inspiration also grants your target 1 additional action.

### Level 15
- [ ] Chorus of Champions — (1/encounter) Free Reaction: Give all party members 1 action.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Taunted — Vicious Mockery (L1), Fight Picker (Herald of Snark L7)
- Movement restricted — Song of Domination (cannot move on next turn)
