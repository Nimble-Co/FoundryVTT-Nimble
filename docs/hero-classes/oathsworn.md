# Oathsworn

**Key Stats:** STR, WIL | **Hit Die:** 1d10 | **Starting HP:** 17
**Saves:** STR+, DEX– | **Armor:** All Armor | **Weapons:** STR Weapons
**Starting Gear:** Mace, Rusty Mail, Wooden Buckler, Manacles

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Radiant Judgment — Whenever an enemy attacks you (with no Judgment Dice), roll 2d6 Judgment Dice. On your next melee hit this encounter, deal that much additional radiant damage. Expended on hit or miss.
- [ ] Lay on Hands — Magical healing pool = 5×LVL; recharges on Safe Rest. Action: Touch a target and spend any amount to restore that many HP.

### Level 2
- [ ] Mana and Radiant Spellcasting — Know Radiant cantrips and tier 1 Radiant spells. Mana pool = WIL+LVL; recharges on Safe Rest.
- [ ] Zealot — Whenever you attack with a melee weapon, spend mana (up to highest unlocked tier) per point spent: Condemning Strike (+5 radiant damage) or Blessed Aim (decrease target's armor by 1 step).
- [ ] Paragon of Virtue — Advantage on Influence checks when forthrightly telling the truth; disadvantage when misleading.

### Level 3
- [ ] Radiant Judgment (2) — Judgment Dice become d8s.
- [ ] Sacred Decree — Learn 1 Sacred Decree.

### Level 4
- [ ] My Life, for My Friends — You can Interpose for free.

### Level 5
- [ ] Radiant Judgment (3) — Judgment Dice become d10s.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 7
- [ ] Master of Radiance — Choose 1 Radiant Utility Spell.

### Level 8
- [ ] Radiant Judgment (4) — Judgment Dice become d12s.

### Level 10
- [ ] Radiant Judgment (5) — Judgment Dice become d20s.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 11
- [ ] Master of Radiance (2) — Choose a 2nd Radiant Utility Spell.

### Level 14
- [ ] Radiant Judgment (6) — Whenever you roll Judgment Dice, roll 1 more.

### Level 18
- [ ] Unending Judgment — While you have no Judgment Dice, gain +5 damage to melee attacks.

### Level 20
- [ ] Glorious Paragon — +1 to any 2 stats. Defend for free whenever you Interpose.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

---

## Choice Abilities — Sacred Decrees

- [ ] Blinding Aura — (1/Safe Rest) Action: Enemies in your aura are Blinded until end of their next turn.
- [ ] Courage! — (1/encounter) When you or an ally in your aura would drop to 0 HP, set their HP to 1 instead.
- [ ] Explosive Judgment — (1/encounter) 2 actions: Expend Judgment Dice; deal that much radiant damage to all enemies in your aura.
- [ ] Improved Aura — +2 aura Reach.
- [ ] Radiant Aura — Action: End any single harmful condition or effect on yourself or a willing creature within your aura. WIL times/Safe Rest.
- [ ] Reliable Justice — Whenever you roll Judgment Dice, roll with advantage (roll one extra, drop the lowest).
- [ ] Shining Mandate — First time each round you are attacked while already having Judgment Dice, select an ally within your aura to roll one and apply it to their next attack. Advantage on checks to see through illusions.
- [ ] Stand Fast, Friends! — When you roll Initiative, grant allies temp HP equal to STR+WIL. You and allies within your aura have advantage against fear and effects that move or knock Prone.
- [ ] Unstoppable Protector — +1 speed. Interpose even if restrained, stunned, or incapacitated. If you Interpose for a non-combatant NPC, may Interpose again this round.
- [ ] Well Armored — Whenever you Interpose, gain temp HP equal to your STR.

---

## Subclass: Oath of Vengeance

### Level 3
- [ ] Aura of Zeal — Whenever you roll Judgment Dice, roll 1 more. Gain an aura with Reach 4. Radiant Judgment also triggers when an ally within your aura is attacked while you have no Judgment Dice.

### Level 7
- [ ] Avenger — Whenever you or an ally within your aura gain Wounds, set up to that many Judgment Dice to their max. Then move up to half your speed for free.

### Level 11
- [ ] Unerring Judgment — Increase primary die rolls on melee attacks by 1 whenever you have Judgment Dice.

### Level 15
- [ ] Maximum Judgment — Whenever you are attacked, set a Judgment Die to its max.

---

## Subclass: Oath of Refuge

### Level 3
- [ ] Aura of Refuge — Your shields gain +WIL armor and count as spellcasting focus. Gain an aura with Reach 4; you can Interpose for an ally anywhere within your aura.

### Level 7
- [ ] Face Me, Foul Creature! — When you Interpose, the attacking enemy is Taunted by you until end of their next turn.

### Level 11
- [ ] Glorious Reprieve — You and allies in your aura cannot drop below 1 HP. Whenever this triggers, they gain 1 Wound instead.

### Level 15
- [ ] Divine Grace — You are resistant to all damage while Interposing.

---

## Auras

- [ ] Aura of Zeal — Reach 4 (Oath of Vengeance L3)
- [ ] Aura of Refuge — Reach 4 (Oath of Refuge L3)

## Conditions Applied

- Blinded — Blinding Aura (Sacred Decree)
- Taunted — Face Me, Foul Creature! (Oath of Refuge L7)
- Prone — Stand Fast, Friends! grants advantage against it (does not apply Prone)
