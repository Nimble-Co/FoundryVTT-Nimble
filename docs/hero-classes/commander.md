# Commander

**Key Stats:** STR, INT | **Hit Die:** 1d10 | **Starting HP:** 17
**Saves:** STR+, DEX– | **Armor:** Mail Armor, Shields | **Weapons:** All Martial Weapons
**Starting Gear:** Short Sword, Javelins, Rusty Mail

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Coordinated Strike! — Gain the Coordinated Strike! Commander's Order.

### Level 2
- [ ] Commander's Orders — Choose 2 Commander's Orders.
- [ ] Field Medic — Roll 1 additional die for any health potion administered. Allies who spend Hit Dice to recover HP (with 10 min examination) add your Examination bonus.

### Level 4
- [ ] Fit for Any Battlefield — Choose a Combat Tactic. When you roll Initiative, gain STR Combat Dice (d6s). (1/attack) Expend a Combat Die to perform a special maneuver. Dice lost when combat ends.

### Level 5
- [ ] Master Commander — When you roll Initiative, regain 1 spent use of Coordinated Strike (lost if unspent). Attacks from Coordinated Strikes ignore disadvantage.
- [ ] Combat Tactics — Combat Dice become d8s.

### Level 6
- [ ] Weapon Mastery — Sheathe and draw a weapon 2×/round for free. Choose a weapon type to specialize in.

### Level 9
- [ ] Combat Tactics (2) — Combat Dice become d10s.

### Level 13
- [ ] Combat Tactics (3) — Combat Dice become d12s.

### Level 17
- [ ] Combat Tactics (4) — Combat Dice become d20s.

### Level 18
- [ ] Unparalleled Tactics — First time each encounter you use Coordinated Strike, an ally who can hear you also gains 1 action on their next turn.

### Level 20
- [ ] Captain of Legions — +1 to any 2 stats. First time each encounter you use Coordinated Strike, EVERY ally within 12 spaces gains +1 action (replaces Unparalleled Tactics).

---

## Choice Abilities — Commander's Orders

- [ ] Coordinated Strike! — (1/round) Free action: You and an ally within 6 spaces both make a weapon attack or cast a cantrip for free. INT times/Safe Rest.
- [ ] Face Me! — Reaction (after an ally is crit within 12 spaces): Taunt that enemy until you drop to 0 HP.
- [ ] Hold the Line! — (1/encounter) Reaction (when an ally drops to 0 HP): Set their HP to 3×LVL.
- [ ] I Can Do This ALL DAY! — (1/encounter) Reaction (when you would drop to 0 HP): Expend Hit Dice and set HP to the sum rolled.
- [ ] Move it! Move it! — When you roll Initiative, give yourself and an ally advantage on the roll and +3 speed for 1 round.
- [ ] Reposition! — Action/Reaction (on an ally's turn): Command 1 ally to move up to their speed (or 2 allies half speed) for free.

---

## Choice Abilities — Combat Tactics

- [ ] Commanding Presence — Action: Shout a command (max 2 words) at an enemy within Reach 12. Failed WIL save (DC 10+STR): they must spend their entire next turn obeying it.
- [ ] Heavy Strike — When you hit, push a Medium creature STR spaces and deal extra Combat Die damage.
- [ ] Inerrant Strike — Reroll a missed attack, add 1 to the Primary Die, deal extra Combat Die damage.
- [ ] Lunging Strike — Gain +1 Reach on an attack and deal extra 2× Combat Die damage.
- [ ] Sweeping Strike — 2 actions: Damage ALL targets in a contiguous area within weapon's Reach. Does not miss on a 1.

---

## Choice Abilities — Weapon Mastery

- [ ] Slashing — Attacks with slashing weapons cannot miss unarmored enemies.
- [ ] Bludgeoning — When primary die rolls 7+ with a bludgeoning weapon, ignore Heavy Armor.
- [ ] Piercing — Attacks with piercing weapons ignore Medium Armor.

---

## Subclass: Bulwark

### Level 3
- [ ] Armor Master — Proficient with plate armor.
- [ ] Shield Expert — While wearing a shield, Defend 2× each round. First time each round you block all damage, make a free opportunity attack against the attacker.

### Level 7
- [ ] Juggernaut — When you use Coordinated Strike, deal extra damage equal to your armor and add 1 to your primary die.

### Level 11
- [ ] Taunting Strike — (1/turn) Taunt a creature you hit until the end of their next turn.

### Level 15
- [ ] Shield Wall — Allies within 2 spaces gain ALL benefits of the shield you have equipped.

---

## Subclass: Vanguard

### Level 3
- [ ] Advance! — (1/round) After moving toward an enemy, advantage on first melee attack against it. When you use Coordinated Strike, you and all allies within 12 spaces can move up to half their speed for free.

### Level 7
- [ ] Experienced Commander — Coordinated Strike may target 1 additional ally. +1 use of Coordinated Strike/Safe Rest.

### Level 11
- [ ] Survey the Battlefield — When you roll Initiative, regain 1 use of Coordinated Strike. +1 max Combat Dice.

### Level 15
- [ ] As One! — Attacks made with Coordinated Strike grant advantage and ignore all disadvantage. Chosen allies gain 1 additional action on their next turn.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Taunted — Face Me! (Commander's Orders), Taunting Strike (Bulwark L11)
- Prone — Heavy Strike can push creatures (implied by pushback)
