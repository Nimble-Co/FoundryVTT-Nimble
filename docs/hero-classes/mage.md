# Mage

**Key Stats:** INT, WIL | **Hit Die:** 1d6 | **Starting HP:** 10
**Saves:** INT+, STR– | **Armor:** Cloth Armor | **Weapons:** Blades, Staves, Wands
**Starting Gear:** Adventurer's Garb, Staff, Soap

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Elemental Spellcasting — You know Fire, Ice, and Lightning cantrips.

### Level 2
- [ ] Mana — Unlock tier 1 Fire, Ice, and Lightning spells. Mana pool = (INT×3)+LVL, recharges on Safe Rest.
- [ ] Talented Researcher — Advantage on Arcana or Lore checks with access to books and study time.

### Level 3
- [ ] Elemental Mastery — Learn the Utility Spells from 1 spell school you know.

### Level 4
- [ ] Spellshaper — Choose 2 Spellshaper abilities (see below).
- [ ] Tier 2 Spells — May now cast and upcast tier 2 spells.

### Level 5
- [ ] Elemental Surge — When you roll Initiative, regain WIL mana (expires at end of combat if unused).
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 6
- [ ] Elemental Mastery (2) — Learn the Utility Spells from a 2nd spell school you know.

### Level 10
- [ ] Elemental Surge (2) — Now regains WIL+1d4 mana.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 14
- [ ] Elemental Mastery (3) — Learn the Utility Spells from a 3rd spell school you know.

### Level 17
- [ ] Elemental Surge (3) — Now regains WIL+2d4 mana.

### Level 20
- [ ] Archmage — +1 to any 2 stats. First tiered spell cast each encounter costs 1 action less and 5 fewer mana.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

---

## Choice Abilities — Spellshaper

- [ ] Dimensional Compression — (1+ mana) +4 range to a spell per additional mana spent.
- [ ] Echo Casting — (2×mana, min 1 mana) When casting a tiered single-target spell, cast a copy on a 2nd target for free.
- [ ] Elemental Destruction — (1+ mana) After hitting with a spell, reroll 1 die per mana spent (up to your WIL).
- [ ] Elemental Transmutation — (1 mana) Change damage type to: Fire, Ice, Lightning, Necrotic, or Radiant.
- [ ] Extra-Dimensional Vision — (2 mana) Ignore line of sight; spell phases through barriers to a target you know within range.
- [ ] Methodical Spellweaver — (–2 mana) Spend 1 additional action to reduce a spell's mana cost by 2 (min 1).
- [ ] Precise Casting — (1+ mana) Choose 1 creature per mana spent to be unaffected by a spell.
- [ ] Stretch Time — (2 mana) Reduce the action cost of a spell by 1 (min 1).

---

## Control Table (Subclass: Control)

- [ ] I INSIST — Cast a cantrip for free, ignoring all disadvantage; it cannot miss.
- [ ] ELEMENTAL AFFLICTION — A creature within 12 spaces gains the Charged, Smoldering, or Slowed condition.
- [ ] NO — Choose a creature; it cannot harm a creature of your choice during its next turn.
- [ ] LOSE CONTROL — Do ALL of the above, but the GM chooses each target.

---

## Subclass: Control

### Level 3
- [ ] Force of Will — (1/round) On your turn, Demand Control: choose 1 option from the Control Table not yet chosen (resets on Initiative or when all options used once).
- [ ] Deny Fate — Whenever you miss with a spell or an effect is saved against, you MUST Demand Control.

### Level 7
- [ ] At Any Cost — Learn 1 cantrip and 1 tiered spell from the Necrotic school.
- [ ] Nullify — (1/encounter) Ignore all disadvantage and negative effects on your next action this turn, then Demand Control.

### Level 11
- [ ] Steel Will — (1/Safe Rest) Whenever you would fail a save, you may succeed instead. Whenever you roll a 1 on an Elemental Surge die, reroll it once.

### Level 15
- [ ] Supreme Control — Whenever you Demand Control, trigger the selected option twice. May Demand Control as a Reaction.

---

## Subclass: Chaos

### Level 3
- [ ] Force of Chaos — Whenever you cast a spell, choose to spend 1 less mana. Whenever you do and whenever you crit, Invoke Chaos: Roll on the Chaos Table (GM-only).

### Level 7
- [ ] Tempest Mage — Learn 1 cantrip and 1 tiered spell from the Wind school.
- [ ] Chaos Lash — (1/encounter) Reaction (when an enemy moves adjacent to you): Push them back 2 spaces; on failed WIL save, also knock Prone. Invoke Chaos.

### Level 11
- [ ] Thrive in Chaos — Whenever you Invoke Chaos, roll twice and cause both effects. (1/Safe Rest) Choose which roll to use instead.

### Level 15
- [ ] Master of Chaos — Whenever you Invoke Chaos, roll with advantage.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Charged — Elemental Affliction (Control Table)
- Smoldering — Elemental Affliction (Control Table)
- Slowed — Elemental Affliction (Control Table)
- Prone — Chaos Lash (Chaos L7)
