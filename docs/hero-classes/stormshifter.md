# Stormshifter

**Key Stats:** WIL, DEX | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13
**Saves:** WIL+, STR– | **Armor:** Cloth or Leather Armor | **Weapons:** Staves, Wands
**Starting Gear:** Cheap Hides, Staff, Strange Plant

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Master of Storms — Know cantrips from the Lightning and Wind schools.
- [ ] Beastshift — Action: Transform into a harmless beast (squirrel, pigeon, etc.). While transformed, speak with animals. Lasts until you drop to 0 HP, cast a spell, or end it on your turn for free. DEX Beastshift charges; reset on Safe Rest.

### Level 2
- [ ] Direbeast Form — Beastshift into a Fearsome Beast (see below).
- [ ] Mana — Unlock tier 1 Wind and Lightning spells. Mana pool = (WIL×3)+LVL; recharges on Safe Rest.

### Level 3
- [ ] Direbeast Form (2) — Beastshift into a Beast of the Pack.

### Level 4
- [ ] Stormcaller — Learn a Utility Spell from each spell school you know.

### Level 5
- [ ] Direbeast Form (3) — Beastshift into a Beast of Nightmares.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

### Level 6
- [ ] Chimeric Boon — Choose 2 Chimeric Boons. Whenever you shapeshift into a Direbeast form, modify it with 1 Chimeric Boon you know.
- [ ] Expert Shifter — Gain 1 additional use of Beastshift per Safe Rest.

### Level 7
- [ ] Stormcaller (2) — Learn a 2nd Utility Spell from each spell school you know.

### Level 8
- [ ] Stormborn — Gain resistance to lightning damage. (1/day) Advantage on a Naturecraft or Concentration check.

### Level 13
- [ ] Stormborn (2) — Instead of rolling dice, deal max damage of a Wind spell by spending a Beastshift charge. Whenever you end Beastshift, cast a cantrip for free.

### Level 20
- [ ] Archdruid — +1 to any 2 stats. (1/encounter) Cast a spell up to tier 4 for free when you enter or leave a Beastshift form.
- [ ] Upgraded Cantrips — Cantrips grow stronger.

## Direbeast Forms

- [ ] Fearsome Beast — (Large) Transform into any Large beast. Gain DEX+LVL temp HP, the Gore attack, and Fearsome. **Gore:** Action: 1d6+LVL damage; on hit gain LVL temp HP. **Fearsome:** Whenever you Interpose or Defend, spend 1 mana to force them to reroll the attack.
- [ ] Beast of the Pack — (Medium) Transform into a Medium beast. Gain +DEX speed, Supercharge, and the Thunderfang attack. Crit/kill gives Thunderfang cumulative +1d4 lightning damage. **Thunderfang:** Action: 1d4+LVL piercing damage. **Supercharge:** Spend up to WIL mana; next Thunderfang deals +1d8 lightning per mana spent (you take this damage on a miss).
- [ ] Beast of Nightmares — (Tiny) Transform into any Tiny beast or insect (horrible). Gain Sting attack and Silent But Deadly. **Sting:** (1/round) Action: Reach 0, 1d4 piercing + 3×LVL acid damage (ignoring armor); crit: 4×LVL instead. **Silent But Deadly:** Speed 2. Cannot Defend or Interpose. Attackers cannot target you until you become conspicuous.

## Choice Abilities — Chimeric Boons

- [ ] Beast of the Sea — Move, breathe, and fight underwater without penalty.
- [ ] Climber — Walk across walls and ceilings; ignore difficult terrain.
- [ ] Fleet Footed — +2 speed. Advantage on Stealth checks and against Grappled condition.
- [ ] Earthwalker — +2 armor. Burrow through dirt and unworked rock at half speed. Advantage against Prone condition.
- [ ] Keen Senses — Advantage on Perception and Assess checks. Unaffected by Blinded.
- [ ] Leader of the Pack — Advantage against fear and charm effects for yourself and allies within 6 spaces.
- [ ] Phasebeast — Whenever you shift between forms, teleport up to 6 spaces away to a place you can see.
- [ ] Prehensile Tail — Creatures you hit in melee that are your size or smaller are Grappled. If you hit a larger creature, move with it when it moves.
- [ ] Winged — Gain a flying speed. Forced movement moves you twice as far while flying.

## Subclass: Circle of Sky & Storm

### Level 3
- [ ] Deepening Study — Choose the Ice or Radiant school to learn.
- [ ] Creature of the Fey — May cast spells while Beastshifted.
- [ ] Attuned to Nature — (1/day) Add LVL to any skill check related to nature or weather.

### Level 7
- [ ] Raging Tempest — Whenever you crit with a tiered spell, cast a cantrip for free from a school you know and haven't cast any spells from this turn.

### Level 11
- [ ] Primordial Force — Spending 2+ mana on a spell grants an additional effect (Ice: gain WIL temp HP; Lightning: deal additional WIL damage; Radiant: heal a creature within 6 spaces WIL HP; Wind: gain flying speed and move 6 spaces for free).

### Level 15
- [ ] Master of Storm — Concentrate on 1 lightning spell and 1 wind spell simultaneously. (1/Safe Rest) Cast Ride the Lightning for 0 mana.

---

## Subclass: Circle of Fang & Claw

### Level 3
- [ ] Swiftshift — When you roll Initiative, Beastshift or move for free. While transformed, shift between Direbeast forms for free (reaction: spend 1 mana). Free Beastshift doesn't grant temp HP.
- [ ] Windborne Protector — (1/encounter) Reaction (when an enemy attacks): Spend 2 mana to shift into a Fearsome Beast, then Interpose from up to 12 spaces away and Defend for free.
- [ ] Friend of Beasts — Beasts won't attack you until you first harm them. Transform into harmless beasts without spending a Beastshift charge.

### Level 7
- [ ] Unleash the Beast — (1/encounter) When you miss, you can crit instead.
- [ ] Storm Wake — (1/encounter) Action: Spend 3 mana to shift into a Beast of the Pack, then teleport in a straight line up to 12 spaces away, dealing WIL d8 lightning damage to chosen creatures adjacent to your path.

### Level 11
- [ ] Master of Forms — Shapeshift forms can have 2 Chimeric Boons at a time.
- [ ] Venomous Gaze — (1/encounter) Action: Spend 2 mana to shift into a Beast of Nightmares, then entice a creature within 12 spaces to move 2×WIL spaces closer on a failed WIL save (disadvantage; repeat until saved or can't move further). If they end in your space, Sting them for free.

### Level 15
- [ ] Master of Forms (2) — Beastshift 2 additional times per Safe Rest. Choose 2 additional Chimeric Boons. Direbeast forms can have 3 Chimeric Boons at a time.
