# Hunter

**Key Stats:** DEX, WIL | **Hit Die:** 1d8 | **Starting HP:** 13
**Saves:** DEX+, INT– | **Armor:** Leather Armor | **Weapons:** DEX Weapons
**Starting Gear:** Shortbow, Cheap Hides, Dagger, Hunting Trap

---

## Core Abilities (by level)

### Level 1
- [ ] Hunter's Mark — Action: A creature you can see is marked as your quarry for 1 day. It can't be hidden from you; attacks against it gain advantage OR +LVL damage (choose per attack).
- [ ] Forager — Advantage on skill checks to find food and water in the wild.

### Level 2
- [ ] Thrill of the Hunt — Choose 2 TotH abilities. Gain a charge whenever your quarry dies or you hit in melee / crit at range against it.
- [ ] Roll & Strike — Action: If you have no TotH charges, move up to speed toward quarry; if adjacent, make a free melee attack.

### Level 3
- [ ] Tracker's Intuition — Discern events of a past encounter from tracks and environmental clues (creatures, direction, actions, time elapsed).

### Level 4
- [ ] Explorer of the Wilds — +2 speed; gain a climbing speed.

### Level 5
- [ ] Hunter's Resolve — Whenever you have no TotH charges, treat ALL creatures as your quarry for movement and melee attacks.
- [ ] Final Takedown — Action: Spend 1 TotH charge to make a melee attack against your Bloodied quarry. Turn it into a crit and double Hunter's Mark bonus damage. If they survive, they crit you back.

### Level 6
- [ ] Versatile Bowmaster — Longbow: roll 2d4 instead of 1d8; Crossbow: roll 2d8 instead of 4d4.

### Level 9
- [ ] No Escape — Whenever allies make an opportunity attack, you may also make a ranged opportunity attack against the same target.

### Level 10
- [ ] Veteran Stalker — Gain a TotH charge whenever first Bloodied in an encounter and for every Wound gained.
- [ ] Keen Eye, Steady Hand — Add WIL to ranged weapon damage.

### Level 13
- [ ] Keen Sight — Advantage on Perception checks.

### Level 17
- [ ] Peerless Hunter — You can Defend against your quarry for free.

### Level 18
- [ ] Wild Endurance — Gain 1 TotH charge at the start of your turns.

### Level 20
- [ ] Nemesis — +1 to any 2 stats. Hunter's Mark can target any number of creatures simultaneously.

## Choice Abilities — Thrill of the Hunt

- [ ] Addling Arrow — Action: Attack with a ranged weapon. The next attack the target makes must be against the closest other creature (random).
- [ ] Come Get Some! — Action: Attack a target. It is Taunted by you until the end of their next turn.
- [ ] Decoy — When you Defend: The attack misses and you move up to half your speed away.
- [ ] Fleet Feet — Move up to your speed for free, ignoring difficult terrain.
- [ ] Grease Trap — (1/encounter) Reaction (when an enemy moves adjacent to you or ally within 6): Target falls Prone, vulnerable to next fire damage, treated as Smoldering.
- [ ] Hail of Arrows — (Half range) 2 actions: Shoot all creatures in a 3×3 area. Speed halved until end of their next turn.
- [ ] Heavy Shot — (Half range) Action: Attack with ranged weapon and push target (4 spaces small, 2 medium, 1 large).
- [ ] Incendiary Shot — (Half range) Action: Attack with ranged weapon, add WIL d8 fire damage.
- [ ] Multishot — (Half range) Action: Attack quarry and select a 2nd target within 2 spaces to take the same damage.
- [ ] Pinning Shot — Spend 3 actions shooting your quarry. They are Restrained until they can escape (DC 10+WIL).
- [ ] Sharpshooter — Action: If you haven't moved this turn and quarry is 4+ spaces away, attack for double damage.
- [ ] Snare Trap — (1/encounter) Reaction (when an enemy moves adjacent to you or ally within 6): Move them back 1 space, Restrained until they escape (DC 10+WIL).
- [ ] Vital Shot — (Half range) Action: If quarry is Hampered, ranged attacks ignore armor. If unarmored, double Hunter's Mark bonus damage.
- [ ] Wild Instinct — (1/round) If you have no TotH charges, assess for free with advantage.

## Subclass: Shadowpath

### Level 3
- [ ] Ambusher — When you roll Initiative, use Hunter's Mark for free. Advantage on first attack each encounter.
- [ ] Skilled Tracker — Advantage on skill checks to track creatures.
- [ ] Skilled Navigator — Cannot become lost by nonmagical means.

### Level 7
- [ ] Primal Predator — (1/encounter) Your weapon attacks ignore cover and armor this turn.

### Level 11
- [ ] Pack Hunter — Whenever you mark a creature, also mark another creature within 6 spaces for free.

### Level 15
- [ ] Apex Predator — Use Primal Predator twice each encounter. Gain 1 TotH charge when you roll Initiative.

---

## Subclass: Wild Heart

### Level 3
- [ ] Impressive Form — +5 max HP. Upgrade Hit Dice to d10s.
- [ ] I Have the High Ground — When you roll Initiative or gain 1+ TotH charges, move up to half your speed for free ignoring difficult terrain.

### Level 7
- [ ] Resourceful Herbalist — When Safe Resting near plants/fungi, spend a day collecting herbs to craft WIL Healing Salves.
- [ ] Healing Salve — Action: Heal yourself or adjacent creature WIL d6 HP. Expires on next Safe Rest.

### Level 11
- [ ] Ha! I'm Over Here! — (1/Safe Rest) If an attack would cause you to drop to 0 HP, move up to speed away and take no damage.

### Level 15
- [ ] Unparalleled Survivalist — Gain +WIL armor. When attacking with ranged weapon, move half speed for free first.

---

## Conditions Applied

- Taunted — Come Get Some! (TotH)
- Prone — Grease Trap (TotH)
- Smoldering — Grease Trap (TotH)
- Restrained — Pinning Shot (TotH), Snare Trap (TotH)
- Slowed (speed halved) — Hail of Arrows (TotH)
- Hampered — referenced by Vital Shot (applied by other sources)
